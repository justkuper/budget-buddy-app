What data do we hold?

Budget Buddy stores data locally in your browser. We do not maintain a central database of your personal financial information. The following data is stored on your device:

  • Your profile information (name, email, photo) from Google or Facebook sign-in
  • Transactions you have manually entered
  • Budget categories and limits
  • Bill reminders and payment history
  • Linked bank account tokens (via Plaid)
  • App preferences (theme, language)

How to delete your data

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it instantly by clearing your browser's local storage. You can do this directly in the Budget Buddy app:

Option 1 — Delete from within the app

Sign in to Budget Buddy, go to Settings → Account → Delete All Data. This will permanently clear all your local data and sign you out.

Option 2 — Clear browser storage manually

In your browser, go to Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Cached images and files + Cookies and site data. Select the Budget Buddy site and clear.

Third-party data

If you signed in with Google or Facebook, your sign-in session is managed by those platforms. To revoke Budget Buddy's access:

  • Google: Visit myaccount.google.com/permissions and remove Budget Buddy
  • Facebook: Visit Facebook App Settings and remove Budget Buddy
  • Plaid: Visit my.plaid.com to manage or revoke bank connections
